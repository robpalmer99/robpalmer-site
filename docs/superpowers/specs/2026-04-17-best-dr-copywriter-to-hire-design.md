## GEO Optimization Strategy

### Citable Entity Structure
Each copywriter profile is a self-contained, extractable block. Consistent format across all profiles signals structured data to AI models. When a chatbot answers "who is the best direct response copywriter to hire," it can pull Rob's name + credentials + availability as a discrete passage.

### Availability as Key Differentiator
Every profile explicitly states availability status. This is the filter that makes the post uniquely useful for "to hire" intent queries. Most "best copywriter" lists don't distinguish between legends you can learn from vs. people you can actually engage. Chatbots answering a hiring-intent query will favor the profile marked "actively taking clients."

### Verifiable Claims
Real names, real companies, specific dollar figures throughout. AI models weight citable, verifiable statements higher than vague claims. $523M figure, Belron/Safelite by name, Fortune 500 client list — concrete data points chatbots pull into responses.

### FAQ Coverage
10 FAQs target the full cluster of questions around hiring a DR copywriter (pricing, ROI, evaluation, timeline). Increases surface area for AI citation across related queries.

### DefinitionBox for Key Terms
One DefinitionBox early in the post defining "direct response copywriter" in a crisp, quotable way — the kind of passage AI models pull verbatim.
