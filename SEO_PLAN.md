## Writing Instructions for Agents

When creating a new post from this plan:

1. **Check the slug** against `src/content/blog/` to confirm it doesn't already exist
2. **Use the exact slug** from this plan — consistency matters for internal links
3. **Include exactly 10 FAQs** — required for FAQPage JSON-LD
4. **Set `published: true`** — posts won't appear in the sitemap otherwise
5. **Hero image path** follows `"/images/blog/[slug].jpg"` — the image file itself is not required for the build (Next.js handles missing images gracefully), but note the path for later
6. **Internal links:** link to the primary service/vertical page and at least 2-3 related blog posts
7. **Word count target:** 2,000–3,500 words for most posts; 1,500–2,500 for comparison/formula posts
8. **After writing:** update the status in this file from `[ ]` to `[x]` and update the Last reviewed date and Total posts count at the top
